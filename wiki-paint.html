Wiki Paint

Collaborative pixel art for your Wikipedia userpage. Anyone who visits can draw — all marks sync in real time.

How to get these:
1. Go to console.firebase.google.com
2. Create a project → Add Web App
3. Enable Firestore Database in test mode
4. Copy your apiKey and projectId from the config snippet

Your credentials stay in the browser. For the iframe embed, add them to the URL:
?key=YOUR_KEY&project=YOUR_PROJECT_ID
